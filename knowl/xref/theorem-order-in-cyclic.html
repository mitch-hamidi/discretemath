Theorem 15.1.13. The order of elements of a finite cyclic group.

If \(G\) is a cyclic group of order \(n\) and \(a\) is a generator of \(G\text{,}\) the order of \(k a\) is \(n/d\text{,}\) where \(d\) is the greatest common divisor of \(n\) and \(k\text{.}\)
